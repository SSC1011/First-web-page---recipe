Easy Chocolate Cookies Recipe

Learn this easy yet classic chocolate chip cookie recipe and enjoy them still warm from the oven with your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 100g Dark Chocolate Chips
  • 100g Milk Chocolate Chips
  • 125g Salted butter
  • 75g Stevia
  • 75g golden caster sugar
  • 1 medium egg, room temperature
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 185g plain flour
  • Â½ tsp bicarbonate of soda

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4 and line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Mix the butter and sugars together until light and fluffy, then add the beaten egg and vanilla.
  3. Once fully mixed, add rest of the ingredients.
  4. Scoop 10 large tbsps of the mixture onto the tray, leaving enough space between each to allow for spreading.
  5. Bake for 10 mins until crispy at the edges but soft in the middle.
  6. Leave to cool on the tray for a few mins before eating warm, ideally with ice-cream.
  7. Store any leftover cookies in a sealed container for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 1 month (be sure to heat them once defrosted).
